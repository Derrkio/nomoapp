NOMO is a SwiftUI iOS music discovery and media archive app that lets users explore artists, browse concert and tour content, play music previews, view embedded media, and upload their own video clips for in-app playback. It was built with Apple frameworks including Swift​UI, AVFoundation, AVKit, and Web​Kit.

AI was used throughout development inside Xcode with ChatGPT/Codex to speed up coding, UI iteration, bug fixing, playback features, upload support, and app styling. Prompt-based development helped shape both the functionality and design of the app.
